Animating the global malaria map

Introduction

In this analytics story, I investigate the prevalence of mosquito-born illnesses and how their distribution may be effected by shifted climate patterns.

In order to tackle this, I first visualised the distribution of malaria over time. Interact with the start and stop animation buttons to view the changing distribution of malaria incidence and deaths over time.

Over the years, the data emphasises three clear hotspots: India, Nigeria and Congo. In the next chapter, I will explore the data further to understand why this is.
